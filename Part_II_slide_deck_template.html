Investigation Overview¶

In this part, I want investigate the varibles effcet the borrower APR and factore effect the monthly loan payment.

Dataset Overview¶

The data set contains 84853 loan with 22 columns information like BorrowerAPR, ProsperScore, MonthlyLoanPayment, credit score, StatedMonthlyIncome and so on.

I created subset of the columns to 22 from 81 columns. looked for duplicate rows. converted datatypes of ListingCreationDate, ClosedDate, DateCreditPulled to datatime and seperated time from columns. Deleted the rows with out prosper score.

Borrower APR effect by prosper rating and term¶

The borrower APR increases when the term increses for B, A and AA rating. The borrower APR decreses when term increases for D, E and C.
